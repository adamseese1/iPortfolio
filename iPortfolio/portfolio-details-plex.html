Home Media Server for my Family

My wife and I both grew up in the 90s so of course we had massive collections of DVDs, CDs and even VHS and Cassettes! Afraid of bit rot, I began backing up all of our media to host via Plex for our own use. After years and years and curating, our library current sits at 44TBs.

This is purely a hobby for me and is one of my favorite ways to spend my free time. While it is a hobby I get valuable experience in Data Management, PowerShell, APIs, Server and Data Management, Powershell, Python, Git, Docker, SSH and Data Backups.
