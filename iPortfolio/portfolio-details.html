Comparing Pass Rush Python Project

As a fun side project after putting the babies to bed, I decided to do some web scraping to gather and plot some data on a defensive players overall impact on games by pulling information on Total Tackles, Sacks, Tackles for Loss, QB Hits, Forced Fumbles and Passes Defensed. I feel as though combining those statistics will truly highlight the impact an all-around player has on the game. I collected the data via scraping with BeautifulSoup and pulled the data I needed and put them into a table. I then created the plot parameters and set colors for every team in the NFL so the dataset could be used for any position or team. Once everything was collected, I used Matplotlib (https://matplotlib.org/) to plot the data in a radar chart to visualize the data set and arranged the subplots for easy comparison.

In gathering this data the overall takeaway is that TJ Watt is an absolute monster of the midway and makes an impact at all levels. Myles Garrett, while impressive in his ability to rush the passer and break the pocket, is clearly not as well-rounded as some of the other stars in the league. Also worth noting that Jadeveon Clowney would not crack the Steelers top 4 most impactful lineman, coming off his best season.
